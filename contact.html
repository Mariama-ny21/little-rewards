Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our rewards system.

  • What is Little Rewards? Little Rewards is a system designed to encourage children aged 4 to 10 to complete tasks and chores through a fun rewards system.
  • How do I sign up? No need to sign up, just visit our homepage and click the "Get Started" button.
  • Are there any costs involved? No, Little Rewards is completely free to use.
  • Can I customize the tasks and rewards? Yes, you can choose from a selection tasks and rewards to suit your child's needs.
  • Is there a mobile app available? Currently, Little Rewards is accessible via our website, but a mobile app may be developed in due course.
