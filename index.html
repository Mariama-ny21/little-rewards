About Little Rewards

Little Rewards is a Task and Reward website focused on children aged 4 to 10. The website has a selection of tasks and rewards that parent can set for their children and gave them the corresponding rewards when the tasks are completed.

By asigning a reward that kids aged 4 to 10 can see on the website, they get excited and motivated in doing the tasks so that they can earn and enjoy their rewards. It allows the kids to be responsible and gives them something to look forward to once tasks are completed.
